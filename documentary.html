across waters

my parents' refugee and immigrant story

For my film class, I directed, filmed, and edited a documentary about my parents. I interviewed my mom and dad about their refugee and immigrant story, and how they navigated life as immigrants in a foreign country. Through this film, I was able to gain a deeper understanding of what my parents had to go through in order to give my sister and I a better life.
