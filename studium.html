Studium

simple communcation between teachers and students

Timeline: Sept - Dec 2021

The Problem

Online learning platforms can be unorganized, hard to use for both students and teachers.

Challenge:

How might we help solve communication with professors and peers for first year university students in their transition phase through a simplified and organized webapp?

The Solution

Studium is an organized, engaging, and all-in-one online learning platform. Within 4 months, I collaborated with 4 other team members to research and build a better educational system for students and professors.

Responsibilities

I was in charge of designing the direct message and video call interface wireframes and mockups on Figma.

Research

To gain a better understanding of what professors and students thought about online learning platforms, we conducted Google form surveys and consulted professors at the Unviersity of Waterloo. We also created user personas and empathy maps of students and professors to better understand our audience.

App Ideation

Based on our research, we came up with features that we wanted to include in our learning platform. We wanted to create a video and voice call feature, as well as direct messaging in order for students to easily communicate with other students and professors.
