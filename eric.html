what's your story?

I recreated Eric's water bottle to communicate his story and represent him as a designer.

Task

For my design class, I was tasked with creating a portrait of my class partner as an Emerging Creative, communicating their design style, their gifts, and their creative origin story.

Outcome

I designed a poster in Eric’s signature style to visually communicate his creative journey. I’ve been friends with Eric for years and I like to think I know his design style really well. To represent his passion for 3D design, I crafted a water bottle out of cardboard, replicating his own. I then wrapped the poster around the bottle, allowing the viewer to physically unravel it, revealing Eric’s story as an emerging creative.
